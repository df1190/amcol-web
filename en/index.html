About Us

Excellence in
Every Inspection

"Total client satisfaction through the delivery of inspection engineering services based on a high level of professional excellence."

We are a private company whose mission is the total satisfaction of our clients through the provision of inspection engineering services and the supply of equipment and materials for the Non-Destructive Testing area, based on a high level of excellence, professionalism and the permanent commitment of our human talent.

We will be a leading company in Colombia in the provision of inspection services and sale of equipment based on Non-Destructive Testing, in the industrial, aeronautical and petrochemical sectors, both nationally and throughout Latin America, applying human, professional and technological standards to contribute to our clients' satisfaction.

Our quality policy is based on the provision of services according to each client's particular needs, fulfilling the established commitments without overlooking efficiency and quality. We count on a qualified human team and recognized suppliers that support material supply and service provision in a timely and effective manner.
